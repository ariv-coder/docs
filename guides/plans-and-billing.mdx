# Plans & Billing

Ringyo AI offers three plans to fit businesses of every size. All plans include a **14-day free trial** with no credit card required.

## Plans

| Feature | Starter | Pro | Agency |
|---------|---------|-----|--------|
| **Price** | $29/month | $79/month | $199/month |
| **Credits** | 500 | 2,000 | 10,000 |
| **Voice Agents** | 1 | 5 | Unlimited |
| **Phone Numbers** | 1 | 3 | 10 |
| **Custom Branding** | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **API Access** | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Priority Support** | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

## How Credits Work

Credits are your usage currency. Different actions consume different amounts:

| Action | Credit Cost |
|--------|------------|
| 1 chat message | 1 credit |
| 1 minute of voice call | 5 credits |
| 1 knowledge base entry | 2 credits |
| 1 appointment booked | 1 credit |

<Tip>Credits reset monthly on your billing date. Unused credits do not roll over.</Tip>

For a deeper dive, see [Credits](/concepts/credits).

## Free Trial

Every new account starts with a **14-day free trial** including 500 credits. No credit card required. You get full access to all features during the trial.

<Warning>When your trial ends, you'll need to choose a plan to continue. Your data and configuration are preserved for 30 days after expiry.</Warning>

## Upgrading & Downgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Dashboard → Settings → Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Select the plan you'd like to switch to and click **Change Plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm changes">
    - **Upgrading:** Takes effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount.
    - **Downgrading:** Takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment Methods

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and process payments securely through Stripe. You can update your payment method anytime from **Settings → Billing**.

## Invoices

Invoices are generated automatically and sent to your account email. You can also download them from **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**.

## Need More Credits?

If you run out of credits mid-cycle, you can purchase **add-on credit packs** from the billing page without changing your plan. See [Credits](/concepts/credits) for details.
