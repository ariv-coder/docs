# Account Setup

Get started with Ringyo AI in under 2 minutes. All you need is an email address.

## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
    Visit [www.ringyo.ai/sign-up](https://www.ringyo.ai/sign-up) and click **Get Started Free**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in your **name**, **email address**, and choose a **password** (min 8 characters). You can also sign up with Google or Microsoft.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from **no-reply@ringyo.ai**. Click the verification link to activate your account.
    <Tip>If you don't see the email, check your spam folder. The link expires in 24 hours.</Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    After verifying, you'll be guided through a quick onboarding flow:
    - Enter your **business name**
    - Select your **business category**
    - Choose your **primary use case** (calls, chat, or both)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Free Trial

Every new account gets a **14-day free trial** with 500 credits — no credit card required. This gives you full access to all features so you can test everything before committing.

<Info>Your trial credits don't roll over. After 14 days, choose a plan to keep using Ringyo AI.</Info>
